Illustrated Environment

Original Photo

Final Illustration

For this assignment, I chose an environment/landscape that brought me back to time of peaceful comfort. I took this picture last year, when me and a friend of mine decided to go apple-picking and hiking upstate. As we walked, we stumbled upon this beautiful, yet simplistic sight of nature. In my illustration, I decided to mantain the original color palette, and instead focus on bringing out the different mountain shapes. I wanted to showcase the infinite shapes we can find if we truly immerse ourself into the image. To do so, I primarily relied on the pen and curvature tools, manipulating the anchors and paths to resemble closely to the actual outline of the picture. I deliberately placed each shape onto a distinct layer, then assigned them a specific shade of green. I used the different options for color gradients to create the cloudy sky, and then layered the original water image to act as a texture for the lake.
